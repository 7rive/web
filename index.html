You shouldn’ t sniff in France. The Frances feel bad when they see the people who sniff in public because a runny nose gets into the body again. The Frances are also worried about the loud sound of sniffing and sneezing.

When you drink alcohol in cafes and restaurants, you don’t make a noise. In France, people think it’s good to recognize the taste of alcohol, but it’s bad to drink alcohol and to make a noise in public.

The festivali started in 1947. The day is a paid holiday when France’s government bans salary cuts and legal labor. Traditional trade union parades are held in big cities.
